# Keynes & Counsel Fintech Advisory Website

## Overview
A professional fintech advisory website for Keynes & Counsel that builds trust and guides users through a clear onboarding process. The site emphasizes transparency, credibility, and simplicity without trading hype or marketing fluff. This is a production-ready website optimized for public deployment on the Internet Computer under the domain name `keynesandcounsel`.

## Design Requirements
- **Visual Style**: Minimal, modern fintech aesthetic with navy, charcoal, and muted green/blue color palette
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts throughout
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with intuitive scroll flow
- **Tone**: Calm, credible, confident messaging
- **Language**: English content
- **Performance**: Optimized for fast loading and smooth user experience
- **SEO**: Production-ready with proper meta tags, structured data, and search optimization

## Page Structure and Content

### 1. Hero Section
- One-line value proposition headline: "Financial Guidance Without the Sales Pitch"
- Subtext explaining "guidance-only, no product pushing" approach
- Primary CTA button: "Get Started for ₹199"
- Secondary CTA button: "How It Works"
- **Background Enhancement**: Apply a semi-transparent dark overlay (rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.45)) or linear gradient from dark navy to transparent over the hero background image to improve text contrast and readability
- **Text Styling**: Ensure white or light text color for optimal legibility across all screen sizes
- Maintain current layout, typography, and hero image aesthetic

### 2. Problem Statement Section
- Icon-based layout with short text blocks
- Illustrates common financial fears and inaction points
- Visual representation of user pain points

### 3. Our Solution Section
- Visual flow diagram showing the service process
- Highlights AI guidance, expert calls, and pay-per-use model
- Emphasizes "no commissions or lock-ins" messaging

### 4. How It Works Section
- Step-by-step process flow: Register → Chat → Call → Action Plan
- Timeline or card-based visual design
- Clear progression indicators

### 5. Trust Section (Why Keynes & Counsel)
- Visual blocks showcasing transparency, low cost, and data privacy
- Trust-building elements and credibility indicators
- Professional presentation without flashy elements

### 6. Pricing Section
- Clear pricing structure: ₹199 registration fee
- Pay-per-call model explanation
- No subscription emphasis
- Transparency and fairness messaging

### 7. Who We Are Section
- Authentic team introduction
- Mission statement emphasis
- Professional but approachable team presentation
- No flashy biographical content

### 8. FAQ Section
- Collapsible question and answer format
- Focus on trust and transparency topics
- Common user concerns addressed

### 9. Final CTA Section
- Calm but clear call-to-action
- "Start your investing journey with clarity" messaging
- Registration encouragement
